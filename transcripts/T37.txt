Full Name: Donald John Trump.​

Birth Date: June 14, 1946.​

Birth Place: Queens, New York City, New York, USA.​

Parents: Frederick Christ Trump and Mary MacLeod Trump.​

Siblings: Maryanne Trump Barry, Frederick Trump Jr., Elizabeth Trump Grau, Robert Trump.​

Education: Attended New York Military Academy (1959–64), Fordham University (1964–66), graduated from Wharton School of Finance at the University of Pennsylvania (B.S. in economics, 1968).​

Height: 6 ft. 3 in. (191 cm).​

Marital Status: Married three times

Ivana Trump (1977–1992)

Marla Maples (1993–1999)

Melania Trump (2005–present).​

Children: Donald Trump Jr., Ivanka Trump, Eric Trump, Tiffany Trump, Barron Trump.​

Business Career: Took over father's real estate business in 1971 (Trump Organization), built hotels, casinos, golf courses, and licensed his brand worldwide.​

Television Career: Hosted reality TV show “The Apprentice” from 2004 to 2015.​

Published Works: Author of several books, including "Trump: The Art of the Deal" (1987), "Crippled America: How to Make America Great Again" (2015), "Think Like a Billionaire," "The America We Deserve," and others.​

Political Career:

45th president of the United States: won 2016 election, served from 2017–21.​

47th president (reelected in 2024; second term began in Jan 2025).​

Republican Party member.​

Historic Impeachments: Only U.S. president impeached twice (2019, 2021); acquitted both times by the Senate.​

Election Outcomes:

Won the presidency in November 2016 (304 electoral votes).​

Lost the 2020 election to Joe Biden (232 electoral votes).​

Other Careers: Acted in several TV shows and movies, such as "Home Alone 2", "Zoolander", and "The Little Rascals".​

Property Holdings: Owns Mar-a-Lago resort in Palm Beach, Florida.​

Notable Legal Events: Convicted of falsifying business records in May 2024; faces trial in Georgia for alleged election interference (pleaded not guilty).​

Personal Preferences: States he abstains from alcohol due to his brother Freddy's alcoholism.​
